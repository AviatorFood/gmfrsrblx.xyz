We are a small group going big. Our main goal is to strive for most professionalised, modern and realistic fire service. We are dedicated to the best roleplays possible through the power of Roblox and our dedicated group members. Our group is not an intention to copy anyone or anything of reality, we are simply trying to bring real life to Roblox to a very good extent. Our Strategic Leadership Team is one of the most professional and active team of people you can find that will be dedicated to a group such as this, they are always here to help and make this community better!


We make everything by hand or is bought from official top quality development groups. We use a lot of our groups robux and some of our own money to purchase the things needed to make this an awesome group.
